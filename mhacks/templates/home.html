About the Project

Playbook is designed for coaches and players who want to enhance their understanding of game plays. With our innovative tool, you can easily upload clips from your favorite games and watch as plays unfold like never before—right on your digital whiteboard.

Key Features

  • Upload Clips: Seamlessly upload your game clips for analysis.
  • Visualize Plays: Experience plays drawn out in real-time on a 2D plane.
  • Pause & Annotate: Coaches can pause plays, make annotations, and highlight key moments.
  • Download Annotations: Easily download your annotations as JPEG images for sharing or reference.
